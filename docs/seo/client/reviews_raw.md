# O'Brien Mountain Home — Raw Google Reviews

This file stores exported Google reviews for use by the SEO Content Engine workflow. Location page briefs use city-specific reviews as social proof. Update this file whenever new reviews come in.

---

## How to Export Your Reviews

**Option 1 — Manual Copy:**
1. Go to your Google Business Profile (maps.google.com → search your business → "View your Business Profile")
2. Click on "Reviews"
3. Copy each review: reviewer name, star rating, date, review text, and the city/neighborhood if mentioned

**Option 2 — Google Business Profile Manager:**
1. Go to business.google.com
2. Select your business → "Read reviews"
3. Screenshot or copy/paste each review

**Format to use below:**
```
---
Reviewer: [First Name, last initial only for privacy]
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)
Date: [Month Year]
Location Mention: [City or neighborhood if mentioned, or "none"]
Review: "[Full review text]"
---
```
